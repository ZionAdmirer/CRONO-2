Welcome to Athens

The cradle of Western civilization, Athens is rich in history with landmarks like the Acropolis and ancient ruins. This vibrant city offers a unique blend of ancient heritage and modern life, making it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking to explore the roots of democracy and philosophy.

Must-Visit Places in Athens

The Acropolis

The most iconic symbol of Athens, this ancient citadel contains several buildings of great architectural and historic significance, including the Parthenon.
